About Toronto Walks

TorontoWalks was created as my capstone project for the Data Science Immersive program at Bitmaker/ General Assembly. I love exploring new areas of the city and hope that this site encourages others to discover something new about Toronto!

TorontoWalks is currently in beta testing. If you notice any bugs, have any questions or suggestions, please drop me an email dataexplorations@outlook.com

The points of interest included in this site were gathered from:

  • ACOToronto WebSite
  • Toronto Plaques
  • Cabbagetown People
  • Toronto Public Art (City of Toronto Open Data)
